Library Policies

    Respect the Quiet: Maintain a peaceful and quiet environment within the library to ensure a conducive space for reading and studying. Use soft voices and avoid disruptive behavior.
    Handle Books with Care: Treat all library materials, including books, magazines, and multimedia, with respect. Avoid marking, folding, or damaging any item. Report any damaged materials to library staff.
    No Food or Drinks: To preserve the cleanliness of the library and protect the books, please refrain from consuming food or beverages inside the library. Use designated areas, such as the caf√© or outdoor spaces, for refreshments.
    Mobile Devices on Silent Mode: Keep your mobile devices on silent or vibrate mode while inside the library. If you need to make or receive a call, please step outside to minimize disruption.
    Return borrowed items on time: Respect due dates and return borrowed items promptly. This ensures that other library patrons have access to the materials they need.
    Respect the staff: Treat library staff with courtesy and respect. They are there to assist you and maintain order in the library. Follow their instructions and guidelines.
    Maintain cleanliness: Keep the library clean and tidy. Dispose of trash in designated bins and report any spills or messes to library staff for prompt cleanup.
    Be mindful of personal belongings: Keep personal belongings with you at all times. The library is not responsible for any lost or stolen items.
    Respect the library's technology resources: Use library computers and other technology resources responsibly and for their intended purposes. Do not engage in any illegal or inappropriate activities.
    Report any concerns or issues: If you notice any maintenance issues, security concerns, or have any suggestions for improvement, please inform library staff so they can address them promptly.
